About Us

India is at the cusp of digital revolution, With MRTechHub, we want to create awareness across industries to make them understand the power of consumer research and the value it can bring to their businesses. India is far behind in terms of doing consumer research, understanding the consumer behavior and analyzing the changing pattern of consumer needs about the products.

MRTechHub platform becomes catalyst and encourage Indian business approach from "DO IT FIRST" to "CONSUMER RESEARCH FIRST". To achieve the end goal of "CONSUMER RESEARCH FIRST", we have created this platform where Business leaders, Research consultants/companies and Research Tech companies can come together, and talk about challenges and opportunities. This eventually will lead industries towards consumer-research based approach. Indian Corporates are still using decades old practices for research, while globally it is totally digitized. India Research Industry has potential to grow multi times, if they can capture consumer behavior faster.
